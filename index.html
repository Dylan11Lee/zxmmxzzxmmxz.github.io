Game Of Life

Rules:

  • Every cell is a life, having 8 neighbours
  • Black means "alive"
  • White means "dead"
  • Cell will die if:
    less than 2 neighbours alive or more than 4 alive, known as "Overpopulation"
  • Cell will become alive if:
    exactly 3 neighbours alive, known as "Miracle"

Instructions:

  • Click "Add" to add some cells you want randomly
  • Click "Start" to simulate the population
  • Click "Stop" to stop the simulation
  • Click "Clear" to stop and clear all cells
